Privacy Policy

Last Updated: March 2026

Website: quickutils.top and all subdomains

1. Introduction

Welcome to QuickUtils. We are committed to protecting your privacy. This Privacy Policy explains how we collect, use, and safeguard information when you visit our website and associated subdomains (collectively, the "Service"). By using the Service, you agree to the practices described herein.

2. Information We Collect

2.1 Data We Do NOT Collect

Our core tools (calculators, formatters, converters, unit tools) run entirely in your local browser via JavaScript. We do not collect, process, transmit, or store any personal data you enter into our client-side tools. No data is sent to our servers.

2.2 Automatically Collected Data

We use the following third-party services that may collect anonymized data:

  • Google Analytics (GA4): Collects anonymized usage data including page views, session duration, approximate geographic location (country/city level), device type, and browser type. IP anonymization is enabled. No personally identifiable information (PII) is collected.
  • Cloudflare Web Analytics: Privacy-first analytics that does not use cookies or collect personal data. Provides aggregate page view and performance metrics.

2.3 Cookies

We use cookies and similar technologies for:

  • Essential Cookies: Theme preferences (dark/light mode) stored in localStorage.
  • Analytics Cookies: Google Analytics uses cookies (_ga, _gid) to distinguish unique visitors. You can opt out via Google's opt-out browser add-on.
  • Advertising Cookies: Google AdSense uses cookies to serve relevant ads. Third-party vendors, including Google, use cookies to serve ads based on your prior visits. You can opt out of personalized advertising at Google Ad Settings or AboutAds.info.

3. How We Use Information

The anonymized data we collect is used exclusively to:

  • Understand traffic patterns and improve site content
  • Monitor site performance and uptime
  • Display relevant advertisements via Google AdSense
  • Improve user experience across our network of directories

4. Third-Party Services

Our Service integrates with the following third-party services:

  • Google AdSense: Displays advertisements. Google may collect data as described in the Google Privacy Policy.
  • Google Analytics: Web analytics service. See Google's Privacy Policy.
  • Amazon Associates: We participate in the Amazon Associates Program. Affiliate links use cookies to track referrals. See Amazon's Privacy Notice.
  • Pinterest: Our automated social media integrations transmit publicly available directory links. We do not collect user data through Pinterest.
  • Cloudflare: Our CDN and hosting provider. See Cloudflare's Privacy Policy.

5. Affiliate Links Disclosure

Some links on our site are affiliate links. When you click on an affiliate link and make a purchase, we may earn a commission at no additional cost to you. This helps fund the free services we provide. For full details, see our Affiliate Disclosure page.

6. Your Rights (GDPR / CCPA)

Depending on your location, you may have the following rights:

  • Right to Access: Request information about what data we hold about you.
  • Right to Deletion: Request deletion of your personal data.
  • Right to Opt-Out: Opt out of data collection via Google's opt-out tools.
  • Right to Rectification: Request correction of inaccurate data.
  • Right to Data Portability: Request a copy of your data in a portable format.

Since we do not collect PII through our tools, these rights primarily apply to analytics and advertising data collected by third-party services listed above. To exercise these rights, contact us at contact@quickutils.top.

7. Data Retention

We do not directly store user data. Third-party analytics data (Google Analytics) is retained for 14 months per Google's default retention policy. Advertising cookies expire according to each ad network's policies.

8. Children's Privacy

Our Service is not directed at children under 13. We do not knowingly collect personal information from children under 13. If you believe a child has provided us with personal information, please contact us at contact@quickutils.top and we will delete such information.

9. Data Security

We use industry-standard security measures including:

  • HTTPS/TLS encryption on all pages
  • Cloudflare DDoS protection and WAF
  • No server-side data storage of user information
  • Regular security reviews and updates

10. Changes to This Policy

We may update this Privacy Policy from time to time. Changes will be posted on this page with an updated "Last Updated" date. We encourage you to review this policy periodically.

11. Contact Us

For any privacy concerns, data requests, or questions about this policy, please contact:

Email: contact@quickutils.top
