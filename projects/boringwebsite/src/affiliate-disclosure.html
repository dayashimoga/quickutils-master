Affiliate Disclosure

Last Updated: March 2026

In short: Some links on QuickUtils are affiliate links. If you purchase through them, we may earn a small commission at no extra cost to you. This helps us keep all our tools and directories free.

Amazon Associates Program

QuickUtils (quickutils.top) is a participant in the Amazon Associates Program, an affiliate advertising program designed to provide a means for sites to earn advertising fees by advertising and linking to Amazon.in and international Amazon stores.

As an Amazon Associate, we earn from qualifying purchases made through affiliate links on our site. The price you pay remains the same whether you use our affiliate link or go directly to Amazon.

How Affiliate Links Work

  • When you click an affiliate link on our site, a cookie is placed on your browser by Amazon.
  • If you make a purchase within the cookie's validity period (typically 24 hours), we receive a small referral commission.
  • The commission does not increase the price you pay for any product.
  • We recommend products we genuinely believe provide value — our recommendations are not influenced by commission rates.

Where You'll Find Affiliate Links

Affiliate links appear in the following areas across our network:

  • Book Recommendations: "Books We Love" sections on the homepage and directory pages
  • Category Pages: Relevant book suggestions paired with API/tool categories
  • Item Pages: Related book recommendations on individual listings

Google AdSense

In addition to affiliate links, QuickUtils displays advertisements through Google AdSense. These ads are served by Google and may use cookies to display personalized content. Revenue from these ads helps fund server costs and development.

Our Commitment

  • We only recommend products and resources that we have personally reviewed or believe are valuable.
  • Affiliate relationships do not influence our directory listings or tool recommendations.
  • All curated content is selected based on quality, usefulness, and community feedback — not commission rates.
  • Free, ad-supported content is our primary model to ensure all users have access regardless of ability to pay.

Earning Globally — How It Works

Currently, QuickUtils is enrolled in the Amazon Associates India program. Here's how we serve users globally:

  • 🇮🇳 Indian Users: Affiliate links point to Amazon.in with our affiliate tag. Purchases generate commission.
  • 🌍 Global Users: We also provide Amazon.com links for international users. While these don't currently generate affiliate revenue, users can still access and purchase books.

Steps to Earn from Global Traffic

To earn from international traffic, site owners can:

  1. Join Amazon Associates in key markets: Apply at Amazon US, Amazon UK, Amazon DE, Amazon JP, etc.
  2. Enable Amazon OneLink: Once approved in multiple markets, enable OneLink which auto-redirects users to their local Amazon store and uses the correct affiliate tag.
  3. Add OneLink script: A single JavaScript snippet auto-localizes all your affiliate links.
  4. Automate with our build system: Our build scripts automatically inject the correct affiliate tags for each market based on OneLink configuration.
